Getting Started

GodMode is your AI assistant that runs 24/7, ready to help you with anything from drafting emails to managing complex projects. Here's how to start using it:

  1. You're already here! The web UI you're looking at right now is your command center. This is where you can chat with GodMode, see your conversations, and manage everything.
  2. Send your first message Try something simple: "Hello! Tell me a joke." or "What can you help me with?" GodMode will respond and you'll be off to the races.
  3. Set up a messaging channel While the web UI is great, the real magic happens when GodMode meets you where you already are - Slack, Telegram, or even iMessage.
  4. Get your Daily Brief Tomorrow morning at 7 AM, your personalized Daily Brief will appear in Obsidian. It's like having a chief of staff prepare your day.

Juggling: Your Superpower

🎪 What is Juggling?

Juggling is GodMode's ability to handle multiple tasks simultaneously across different conversations and channels. While you're chatting about one thing, GodMode can be working on five other things in the background.

Think of it like having a team of assistants, except they all share the same brain and context about you.

How Juggling Works

When you give GodMode a task, you don't have to wait for it to finish before starting something else:

Parallel Processing

Ask GodMode to research competitors while it's also drafting your presentation. Both happen at the same time.

Cross-Channel

Start a task in Slack, continue it via Telegram, finish in the web UI. GodMode remembers everything.

Background Tasks

"Monitor this and let me know when it changes." GodMode watches while you do other things.

Shared Context

Results from one task automatically inform others. Research you did yesterday helps with today's writing.

Using GodMode Securely

GodMode is designed with security in mind. Here's what you need to know:

✓ Self-Hosted

GodMode runs on your own infrastructure. Your data never passes through third-party servers (except the AI providers you choose).

✓ Token Authentication

Access to your GodMode requires a secure token. Never share this token publicly.

✓ Tailscale Encryption

When using Tailscale, all traffic is encrypted end-to-end. Your GodMode is only accessible on your private network.

✓ API Key Security

Your API keys are stored in environment variables, never in code or logs.

✓ No Training on Your Data

Anthropic (Claude) does not train on API data. Your conversations are private.

✓ Audit Trail

All actions are logged. You can see exactly what GodMode has done.

Best Practices

  1. Use strong, unique tokens Your gateway token is your key to GodMode. Treat it like a password.
  2. Keep Tailscale connected This ensures your GodMode is only accessible from your trusted devices.
  3. Review connected services Periodically check what services GodMode has access to and revoke any you no longer need.
  4. Use 1Password for secrets Store your API keys and tokens in 1Password, not in plain text files.

Pro Tips

Talk to GodMode like a smart colleague. "Can you..." works better than robotic commands.

"For my meeting with investors tomorrow..." helps GodMode understand the stakes and tone.

🔄 Iterate

"Make it shorter" or "More professional" - refine outputs with quick feedback.

⚡ Don't Wait

While GodMode works on one thing, ask for something else. Juggling is your superpower.

📝 Use Templates

"Write this in my usual email style" - GodMode learns your preferences over time.

🎯 Be Specific

"Draft a 3-paragraph email" is better than "write an email" for predictable results.
