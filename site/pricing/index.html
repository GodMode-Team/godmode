Frequently asked questions

What's a seat?

One seat = one user. If you have a team, add a seat for each member who needs access.

Can I cancel anytime?

Yes. Cancel from your account dashboard at any time. Your access continues through the end of the current billing period.

What AI models are supported?

GodMode works with any Claude subscription -- Pro, Max, or your own API key. Bring your existing plan.

What do I need to get started?

A Mac or Linux machine (or a VPS) and a Claude subscription. The install script handles everything else automatically.
